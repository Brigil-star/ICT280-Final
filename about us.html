Company Story

GreenTech Solutions was founded in 2015 with the single aim of providing sustainable energy solutions for everyone. From a small team of passionate environmental engineers in 2015, we have grown exponentially to become one of the leading solar technology solution providers in the country with over thousands of satisfied customers from all across the nation.

Our Mission

We at GreenTech Solutions believe that by providing clean energy not only will we be helping the environment, but also will be providing long term financial benefits for the individual and business alike.
